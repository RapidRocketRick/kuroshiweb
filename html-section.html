Our Home

Kuroshi is a clan originating from Roblox's ZO, a samurai sword-fighting game. However, our main target is not only ZO, but also to gain more members, provide a fun community rather than a strict one that's based on game ranking. We look forward to spanning over more games, to make fun events, to have an enjoyable YouTube channel, and more. You can help us too, by joining our clan on Discord, Roblox and subscribing to the YouTube channel!
